Homepage

How to use:

  1. Go to Register Employee Tab
  2. Enter Name and postion of Employee
  3. Go to Calculate Salary Tab
  4. Enter month of payout
  5. Select position of employees you want to assign salary to
  6. Go to View Payout Tab
  7. Select the month of the payout to view

Features:

  • Under the Edit Records Tab, you can double click any value you want to make changes to
  • When Assigning Trips for employees, you can double click the added trips to remove any trips from the queue
  • When viewing payouts, you can double click the name of any employee to view the employees individual record for the month

Please remember to export your session date before closing your browser!
